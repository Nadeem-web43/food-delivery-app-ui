# Problem Statement

People often struggle to order food quickly because many food delivery apps have complicated navigation, slow ordering processes, and limited personalization. Users want an application that makes discovering restaurants, exploring menus, placing orders, and tracking deliveries simple and efficient. This Food Delivery App is designed to provide a smooth, user-friendly, and visually appealing experience that reduces the time and effort required to order food online.

# Why is this App Needed?

* To provide a fast and convenient food ordering experience.
* To help users discover nearby restaurants and cuisines.
* To simplify menu browsing and food selection.
* To reduce the time taken to place an order.
* To provide a secure and easy checkout process.
* To improve the overall user experience with a modern interface.
* To allow users to track their orders and receive delivery updates.

# Target Users

* Students looking for affordable and quick meals.
* Working professionals with busy schedules.
* Families ordering meals from home.
* People who prefer online food delivery instead of dining out.
* Restaurants that want to reach more customers through digital platforms.

# Project Goals

* Design a clean and modern mobile user interface.
* Make food ordering simple and intuitive.
* Improve usability through easy navigation.
* Reduce the number of steps required to place an order.
* Create a responsive and visually attractive design.
* Ensure accessibility for users of different age groups.
* Build a professional UI/UX portfolio project suitable for an internship.

# User Pain Points

* Difficult-to-use interfaces.
* Too many steps during checkout.
* Slow navigation between screens.
* Poor food search and filtering options.
* Lack of clear order tracking.
* Confusing menu layouts.
* Limited payment and delivery options.
* Inconsistent user experience across different screens.

# Features Users Want

* Quick and secure login.
* Easy account registration.
* Search for food and restaurants.
* Browse food categories.
* View detailed food information.
* Add items to the shopping cart.
* Manage cart quantity.
* Multiple payment options.
* Real-time order tracking.
* Save favorite restaurants and dishes.
* Order history.
* Notifications for order status.
* User profile management.
* Ratings and reviews.
* Fast and simple checkout process.
